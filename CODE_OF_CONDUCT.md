# Code of Conduct

This project is committed to a respectful, constructive and inclusive open-source community.

## Expected behaviour

- Be kind and professional.
- Assume good intent.
- Give actionable feedback.
- Respect different levels of experience.
- Keep discussions focused on the project.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behaviour
- Publishing private information
- Spam or bad-faith disruption

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict participation where needed to protect the community.
